AFROFUTURISM 101

Afrofuturism, a cultural phenomenon commonly associated with 'Black Panther'. Not many people know of the vast history of Afrofuturism, as well as the other genre's that its roots have touched.

It has been used as a lens which to focus in on black history, scrutinising systemic oppression and racial injustice, while using technology as a vehicle to envision a truly libering future.
